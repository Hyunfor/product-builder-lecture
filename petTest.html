My Pet's Face Test

Curious to see if your pet's face is more like a cat or a dog? Upload an image of your pet's face and let our machine learning model analyze it for you! This tool is based on Google's Teachable Machine and provides a fun prediction.

How to use:

  1. Click "Choose File" and select a clear image of your pet's face.
  2. Once the image appears, click the "Analyze" button.
  3. See the results below!

Disclaimer: This is for entertainment purposes only and is not a scientific analysis. The model's accuracy may vary.
